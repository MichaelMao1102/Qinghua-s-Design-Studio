QINGHUA'S DESIGN STUDIO

The website's logo and webpage selected orange as the color tone, because the brand's founder, Qinghua Mao, loves Canada's beautiful autumn with its maple leaves. Autumn symbolises that all the hard work will yield results. The selection of all images on the webpage creates a harmonious color tone with orange. My vision was that users can be attracted at first sight by the website and interested in the design services.
